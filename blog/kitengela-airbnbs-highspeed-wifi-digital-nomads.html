What Remote Workers Must Check Before Booking

To get the most out of your workspace airbnb kitengela, always double-check these three features with your host before making a payment:

  • Power Backup Solutions: Solar systems, automatic generators, or heavy-duty inverters are non-negotiable if you cannot afford down-time during local power grid maintenance.
  • Internet Quality: Ask your host for a recent speed-test screenshot. Ensure the network is unlimited fibre rather than a restricted mobile data hotspot.
  • Desk Comfort: Working from a soft bed or a low coffee table for eight hours will ruin your posture. Ensure the listing features an actual desk and a supportive, comfortable chair.
