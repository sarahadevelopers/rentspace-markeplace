Crucial Features to Verify Before Booking a Monthly Rental

An apartment that is perfect for a weekend staycation might feel restrictive after three weeks. Before committing to an extended stay kitengela, ensure you confirm these three lifestyle features with your host:

  • Comprehensive Kitchen Infrastructure: Ensure the kitchen features a full‑sized cooker, oven, large fridge, and a wide selection of cookware. Microwaves and small kitchenettes are rarely enough for month‑long home cooking.
  • Housekeeping and Linens: Clarify how often the apartment will be cleaned and how frequently fresh bedsheets and towels will be provided. Most long‑term bookings include structured weekly cleaning schedules.
  • Storage and Wardrobe Space: Solo weekenders live out of suitcases, but monthly guests need deep closets, drawers, and hangers to keep clothes neat and well‑maintained.

Frequently Asked Questions (Long‑Term Stays)

What is the average monthly rate for a furnished apartment in Kitengela?

For a 1‑bedroom furnished apartment, monthly rates range from KES 25,000 to KES 45,000 depending on location and amenities. 2‑bedroom units range from KES 40,000 to KES 70,000 per month.

Do monthly rentals include utilities?

Many do – but always confirm. Most of our long‑term listings include water, Wi‑Fi, and security fees. Electricity may be metered separately or capped.

Can I get a discount for booking 3+ months?

Absolutely. Many hosts offer additional discounts for extended bookings beyond one month. Contact us for a custom long‑stay quote.
