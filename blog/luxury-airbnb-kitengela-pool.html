Premium Features to Look for Before Booking

To ensure your high-end stay matches your expectations, always verify that your premium booking includes these essential features:

  • Uninterrupted Power & Water: True luxury means never dealing with local utility cuts. Ensure the property lists automatic generator backups and large-capacity borehole water storage.
  • Top-Tier Security Architecture: Premium properties should feature multi-layer security, including biometric access control, 24/7 manned security guards, CCTV monitoring, and secure, private parking structures.
  • Bespoke Services: Many elite hosts offer add-on options like private chefs, airport transfers via the Expressway, or pre-stocked grocery fridges to make your stay effortless.
