Moving to Kitengela? The Honest Guide to Rents, Water, and Commutes (2026)

May 1, 2026 8 min read 3478 views

Are you considering moving to Kitengela? Known for its **affordability**, **growing infrastructure**, and **family-friendly environment**, Kitengela is rapidly becoming a sought-after residential hub. Located only 30 kilometers from Nairobi’s CBD, Kitengela is an attractive choice for families and professionals alike.

Rental Market Snapshot: Kitengela (2026)

Understanding the cost of living and rental prices is essential for anyone planning to move to Kitengela. Here's a breakdown of typical rental costs in the area:

Water Supply in Kitengela

One challenge to consider when renting in Kitengela is water supply. Approximately **40% of households** have access to piped water. In many gated communities, however, **borehole desalination systems** are used to ensure a reliable water supply. Make sure to ask about water sources and storage when selecting a rental property in Kitengela.

The Commute Reality: Kitengela to Nairobi

The commute from Kitengela to Nairobi's CBD is often a hot topic. Off-peak traffic can take **25-40 minutes**, but during peak hours, it can stretch to over **90 minutes**. Thankfully, the recent improvements in the **Nairobi Expressway** and the **Kitengela-Nairobi commuter train** have eased travel times considerably.
