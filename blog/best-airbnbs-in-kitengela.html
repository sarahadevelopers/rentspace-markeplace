Top 10 Best Airbnbs in Kitengela for a Perfect Staycation

May 20, 2026 12 min read 1,528 views

Escaping the fast-paced rush of Nairobi no longer requires driving for hours or breaking the bank. Located just a short drive down Mombasa Road, Kitengela has quickly transformed into one of the most vibrant staycation hubs near the city. Whether you are looking to unwind after a long workweek, enjoy the region's famous local nyama choma, or find a quiet space to clear your mind, the town offers incredible hidden gems.

In this comprehensive guide, we review the best airbnbs in Kitengela that offer the perfect blend of comfort, style, security, and top-tier amenities for an unforgettable kitengela staycation.

Why Choose a Kitengela Staycation?

Kitengela offers a unique getaway experience. The weather is generally warmer than Nairobi, making it perfect for afternoon swimming. It is incredibly accessible via the Nairobi Expressway, allowing you to beat the classic Mombasa Road traffic and arrive at your destination in minutes. Furthermore, choosing places to stay in Kitengela gives you luxury-level comfort at a fraction of the price you would pay in city centre suburbs.

Key Factors to Consider When Booking a Kitengela Airbnb

When searching for furnished apartments Kitengela, keep these three local tips in mind to ensure a smooth stay:

  • Water Supply: Kitengela is known for water challenges. Always confirm your host has reliable borehole water backup and overhead storage tanks.
  • Security: Prioritise properties located within secure estates that feature 24/7 manned gates, CCTV surveillance, and secure perimeter fencing.
  • Proximity to the Highway: If you do not want to navigate rough feeder roads, choose listings located close to Namanga Road or Kitengela Town centre.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) About Stays in Kitengela

How much does an average Airbnb in Kitengela cost?

Prices range from KES 2,500 to KES 4,500 per night for cosy studio and 1-bedroom apartments. Larger luxury apartments or villas with swimming pools range between KES 6,000 and KES 12,000 per night.

Is Kitengela safe for a weekend staycation?

Yes. The town is very welcoming, and the holiday rentals featured on our platform are situated within highly secure, gated residential zones with round‑the‑clock guards.

How far is Kitengela from Nairobi CBD?

It is approximately 30 kilometres away. By using the Nairobi Expressway from CBD to the Mlolongo exit, the drive takes only 30 to 45 minutes depending on minor traffic holds.
