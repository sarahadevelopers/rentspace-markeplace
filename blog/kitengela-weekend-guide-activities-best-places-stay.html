Kitengela Weekend Guide: Fun Activities and the Best Places to Stay

May 20, 2026 10 min read 1,984 views

For a long time, many people viewed Kitengela simply as a busy residential town on the outskirts of Nairobi. Today, it has evolved into one of the most exciting choices for a quick, action‑packed kitengela weekend getaway. The town offers a brilliant mix of artistic wonders, high‑adrenaline outdoor parks, and a world‑famous culinary scene. It provides an ideal break from city life without requiring days of travel planning.

If you are planning a Saturday and Sunday escape, compiling an itinerary of the best things to do in kitengela ensures a memorable trip. Pair these adventures with premium kitengela accommodation for the perfect weekend experience.

Top Things to Do in Kitengela on a Weekend

1. Explore the Whimsical Kitengela Hot Glass Studio

Tucked away on the edge of Nairobi National Park near Oloosirkon is Anselm's famous glass studio. This architectural wonderland lets you watch master artisans melt recycled bottles and window glass into stunning, colourful sculptures.
The Highlights: Walk across the famous, adrenaline‑pumping suspension bridge dangling over a deep river gorge, or sign up for a hands‑on glassblowing experience to create your own souvenir.
Pro‑Tip: Watching the live studio glassblowing demonstrations is completely free! The studio runs from 8:00 AM to 4:30 PM Tuesday through Saturday.

2. Conquer the Obstacle Courses at Nkasiri Adventure Park

If you are looking for an active, high‑energy afternoon, head out to Nkasiri Adventure Park. Located roughly 45km from Nairobi CBD, this thrill‑seeker's paradise is perfect for groups of friends, couples, and active families.
The Highlights: Test your fitness on the multi‑tiered high ropes courses, fly through the air on the zipline, or try your hand at archery and paintballing. The park also features a refreshing outdoor swimming pool to cool off afterward.

3. Indulge in World‑Famous Kitengela Nyama Choma

No trip to this region is complete without sample‑tasting its signature delicacy. Kitengela is legendary across Kenya for serving some of the juiciest, most expertly grilled nyama choma (roasted meat).
The Highlights: Popular open‑air joints scattered along Namanga Road serve fresh, tender goat meat paired with hot ugali and kachumbari (tomato and onion salad). It is the ultimate weekend afternoon social ritual for locals and visitors alike.

4. Family Fun at Kids' Amusement Parks

If you are traveling with children, Kitengela features excellent, dedicated family fun centers. Hotspots like GMC Place Kitengela and Rayan Park offer massive children's amusement parks filled with water slides, swimming pools, bouncy castles, and family‑friendly restaurants.

5. Safari Walk & Nature Trails (Nearby National Park)

For nature lovers, the proximity to Nairobi National Park allows for morning game drives or a visit to the Safari Walk where you can spot lions, rhinos, and giraffes just minutes from your Airbnb.

Frequently Asked Questions (Weekend Guide)

How far is Kitengela from Nairobi?

Approximately 30 kilometres via Mombasa Road. Using the Expressway, you can be there in 30‑45 minutes outside peak traffic hours.

What is the best time to visit Kitengela for a weekend getaway?

The weather is warm year‑round, but the dry seasons (January‑February and June‑October) are ideal for outdoor activities.

Can I do a day trip from Nairobi to Kitengela?

Absolutely. However, staying overnight allows you to fully relax and experience the evening nyama choma scene without rushing back.
